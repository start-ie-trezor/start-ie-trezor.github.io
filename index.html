Welcome to Trezor Setup

Discover how to set up your Trezor hardware wallet with the latest version (22.9.3). Ensure your cryptocurrency is protected with cutting-edge security features.

Setup Instructions

Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Unbox your Trezor hardware wallet.
  • Connect it to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  • Visit Trezor.io/start to begin the setup process.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  • Ensure to back up your recovery seed in a secure location.

Why Choose Trezor?

The Trezor hardware wallet offers unmatched security for your cryptocurrency. With advanced encryption and user-friendly features, you can be confident that your assets are safe.

FAQs

What is Trezor.io/start?

Trezor.io/start is the official website where you can set up and configure your Trezor hardware wallet.

How do I update my Trezor firmware?

Visit the Trezor.io/start page and follow the instructions to update your firmware to the latest version.

Can I use Trezor with other cryptocurrencies?

Yes, Trezor supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies beyond Bitcoin, including Ethereum and many altcoins.
